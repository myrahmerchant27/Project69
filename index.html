Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

The first church of St. Francis Xavier was constructed in 1851 by French missionaries of the Paris Foreign Missions Society. Due to the huge increase in the catholic population, this church eventually became inadequate. Hence in 1911, the foundation stone was laid for a new church.



School Library

Was built in 1980

The Library exists to facilitate and to assist the investigation of human knowledge and the acquisition of research skills for both teachers and students. It is our belief that the future welfare of our students is as good as the quality of information available to them.



School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

The school auditorium has been designed to be the centre stage of Inter-School competitions, ceremonies, lectures by personalities from varied fields of music & drama, dance performances, competitions, and numerous other fun-filled and elating events and can accommodate 200+ people.
